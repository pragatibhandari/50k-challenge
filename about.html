My Story — The Challenges That Define Me

🎓 The University Challenge - show up everyday

Mission: Graduate with honors

I believe growth begins where comfort ends. In October 2024, I moved to Vaasa to begin my master's in Automation Engineering, specializing in Robotics, at the University of Vaasa. With one suitcase, limited resources, and a heart full of determination, That’s when I launched what I called the "University Challenge" - a personal commitment to show up every day, study hard, and aim to graduate with honors.

I began filming 🎥 one-minute daily videos to document the journey every single day, not for validation, but to stay consistent, grounded, and connected to my purpose.

That challenge is now paused for summer. And a new one has begun. 🌞⏸
